Introduction

Raw Stone Jewlery

Raw Stone Jewlery is a contemporary jewelry brand based on the idea that minimalism brings out the essence of design and craft. Every piece is handcrafted in San Luis Obispo, Ca, focusing on historical design and traditional metalsmithing techniques.

Target Audience

Each piece is individually handcrafted, mostly of fine and reclaimed silver and gold, with precious and semi-precious stones. Raw Stone Jewlery also incorporates bronze, brass and copper by using innovative metalsmithing techniques to create original shapes, textures and subtle color tones.

Raw Stone Jewlery is here to make you and your loved ones jewelry that’s unique to you and your stories. Every order is personal and crafted specifically for you. Every Raw Stone Jewelry piece tells its own unique, nature-inspired story, providing a lifetime of wearable enjoyment.
